Our Story

The Woman Behind the Thread

Manju's Story

"I never thought of it as embroidery. To me, it was always painting — just with a needle and thread instead of a brush." — Manju, Founder

The Beginning

A Childhood Painted in Thread

Long before Keep It Stylish existed, there was a young girl in a small Indian city who spent her afternoons doing something quietly extraordinary. While others played, Manju painted — not on paper, but on fabric. Needle in hand, thread after thread, she created intricate pictures that her family still remembers vividly.

She was entirely self-taught. No art school, no mentor, no formal training. Just an instinctive eye for colour, a steady hand, and an obsession with texture that would later become the soul of her brand.

The Journey

Building Something of Her Own

Running a business as a woman in a small city is not easy. The obstacles are real — limited resources, limited networks, and a persistent current of doubt from those who cannot yet see your vision. Manju felt all of this and built anyway.

She started with custom orders — one at a time, word of mouth, each piece better than the last. Keep It Stylish was born from that foundation: the belief that every woman deserves clothing made with the same attention she would give it herself.

The Craft

What Makes Every Piece Different

At Keep It Stylish, garments are not manufactured. They are made. Manju's background in thread embroidery informs everything — the weight of the embellishment, the way a motif is placed, the choice of fabric that will carry the work without losing its drape.

Our speciality is the space between Indian tradition and contemporary wearability. Silhouettes you can move in. Craftsmanship that lasts. Clothing that feels like it was made for you — because often, it was.
